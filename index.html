About Canovet

At Canovet, we understand that pets are family. Our team of experienced veterinarians and pet care specialists is dedicated to providing the highest quality care for your beloved companions.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Sarah Johnson, a passionate veterinarian with over 15 years of experience, Canovet began as a small neighborhood clinic with a big dream: to revolutionize pet care by making it more accessible, comprehensive, and compassionate.

What started as a single clinic has grown into a trusted network of pet care professionals, serving thousands of pets and their families across the region. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us the go-to choice for pet owners who want nothing but the best for their furry companions.

Today, we're proud to offer a full spectrum of services, from routine checkups to emergency care, all delivered with the same level of dedication and expertise that has defined us from the beginning.
